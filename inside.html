About Us

History

WWW.LANDA.LV Is a small family business located in the territory of Latvia

We work with natural leather and create various leather accessories according to our customer's requirements.

All our accessories are unique because they are completely handmade and we have a wide range of them.

The accessories are made of cattle's hide which is tough and the stitching is done with waxed thread, which makes it durable.We provide these accessories to retailers as well as individuals.

Clients can customize the accessories according to their preferences.

It is important for us to see a satisfied customer.
